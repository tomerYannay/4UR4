# Workflow — Status Report

A short, regular status report keeps the human oriented with **minimal process
overhead** (requirement 10). Authored by the **Project Auditor**; it reports facts,
not opinions.

## Cadence
Once per cycle (and on request).

## Contents (use [`templates/status-report.md`](../templates/status-report.md))

- **Verified progress** — tickets reaching evidence-backed Done this cycle
  ([GOV-009](../governance/prioritization.md)).
- **In flight** — `in-progress` / verification / review, with age.
- **Throughput vs volume** — Done-with-evidence vs opened tickets.
- **Budgets** — WIP, ticket, idea counts against caps ([GOV-008](../governance/ticket-hygiene.md)).
- **Roadmap** — current Now/Next/Later and any human-approved changes.
- **Risks & violations** — governance breaches, drift, stale/blocked items.
- **Freeze state** — build-freeze status and any pending approval requests.
- **Ideas** — new cards, triage outcomes.
- **Ask of the human** — the specific approvals or decisions needed next.

## Rules

- **One page.** If it needs more, the system is doing too much
  ([GOV-008](../governance/ticket-hygiene.md)).
- Every claim of progress **links to evidence**; unproven progress is reported as
  *unverified* ([GOV-006](../governance/definition-of-done.md)).
- The report **requests**, never assumes, human approvals
  ([GOV-013](../governance/approval-gate.md)).
